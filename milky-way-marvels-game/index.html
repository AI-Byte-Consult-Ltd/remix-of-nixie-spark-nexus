Game Logic

You pilot a collector ship through a solar storm. Catch NFT-style planets and cosmic objects, avoid asteroids and black holes, keep your fuel alive, and build a combo streak.

  • Planets: +100 score and +1 combo.
  • $RAVERSE crystals: +25 score and refill a little fuel.
  • Fuel cells: restore fuel.
  • Asteroids: break combo and damage your ship.
  • Black holes: heavy damage and gravity pull.
  • Boost: move faster, but burns fuel.

Local leaderboard is saved in the browser. Web3 leaderboard can be added later using Ebisu's Bay sales/API data or wallet snapshots.
